=== Description ===
Insurify App allows merchants like yourself to offer insurance protection on the products or services you sell. You can start covering stolen or broken items that have been insured by the consumer. 

Insurify increases customer confidence at a pivotal point in the purchase process, resulting in site conversion increase.

Shoppers can buy with confidence knowing their purchase is guaranteed. Insurify reinforces trust in your brand and aids in purchasing decisions at the checkout page.

Allow your customers to insure their shipments in one-click Increase conversion, customer loyalty, and lifetime value Increase trust by guaranteeing shipments.
